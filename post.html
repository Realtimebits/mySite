SEO Types

There are different parts of Search Engine Optimization that need individual attention. Technical SEO: The technical SEO takes care of how your page ranks in search engines (like Google, Yahoo, etc.) after a user enters a search. It enables the search engines to crawl and gather idea and info on your page and store it in database so it can show it in search results. Technical SEO includes:
  • Efficient Coding
  • Speed of the Website
  • Secured URL with SSL/HTTPS
  • Mobile SEO

Organic SEO

Organic SEO involves working on the “organic area” to ensure your web pages rank in the initial listings. It helps to reach your clients in the domestic and international markets.

Page SEO

It deals with optimization of elements on the visible page to affect its rankings. Organic SEO includes:
  • Web page meta description
  • Web page title
  • Unique body copy or content on each page
  • Headings

Local SEO

Local SEO targets local or regional consumers and is best for businesses targeting at certain regions or local areas.

Off-Page SEO:

It targets and optimizes elements that are not connected to the page. It links to pages on your site from other websites.
  • Backlinks
  • Social Media
  • Guest blogging
  • Boosting branded searches
  • Growing engagement and social media sharing

Voice SEO

Voice SEO improves voice search rankings. This is the latest type of SEO and is expected to take over 50% of search types by the end of 2020.
